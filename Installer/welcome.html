SIRIUS.video Virtual Audio Driver

This virtual audio driver allows you to pass audio from SIRIUS.video to other applications with zero additional latency.

Features

  • 2ch version.
  • 8k, 16k, 44.1kHz, 48kHz, 88.2kHz, 96kHz, 176.4kHz, 192kHz, 352.8kHz, 384kHz, 705.6kHz and 768kHz sample rates.
  • No driver latency.
  • Runs on macOS 10.9+ including macOS Big Sur
  • Built for Intel and Apple Silicon.
