FAQ

Where is SIRIUS.video Virtual Audio Driver located?

You can find it in Audio Midi Setup and within audio application.

Why is nothing playing through SIRIUS.video Virtual Audio Driver?

  • Check System Preferences -> Security & Privacy -> Privacy -> Microphone to make sure your audio application has microphone access.
  • Check that the volume is all the way up on SIRIUS.video Virtual Audio Driver output in Audio Midi Setup.
  • If you are using a multi-output device, due to issues with macOS a 2ch audio driver must be enabled and listed as the top device in the Multi-Output.
